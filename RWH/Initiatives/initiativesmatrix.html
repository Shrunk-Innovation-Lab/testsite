Now Your Turn! (8 minutes)

Your Worksheet Has 3 Ideas

Work together to score each one. There are no wrong answers!

1

Read the idea

What is it asking us to do? Does everyone understand it?

2

Answer the 4 questions

Time? Cost? Effort? Emissions impact? Score each dimension.

3

Decide together

Should we do this? Why or why not?

ü§ù Remember:

Everyone's opinion matters! Clinical staff, admin, facilities ‚Äì you all see different parts of the hospital.

üí≠ Questions to Ask Each Other:

Which departments would be involved? Do we already have what we need? Have we tried something like this before?

The facilitator will help if you get stuck ‚Äì just ask!

Share Your Thinking (5 minutes)

Let's Hear From Each Group

Each group shares their scores and reasoning for one initiative

üìä Presentation Format

Pick ONE initiative from your worksheet to present. Share your scores across all 4 dimensions and explain your reasoning.

What to Share

  • Your scores for each dimension
  • Why you scored it that way
  • Where it plots on the matrix
  • Would you recommend doing it?

Discussion Points

  • Did groups score the same initiative differently?
  • What factors influenced your thinking?
  • Did you consider factors beyond the 4 main dimensions?

üí° Remember

There's no single "right" answer ‚Äì different departments may score initiatives differently based on their knowledge and perspective. That's valuable!

You've Got The Basics!

What You Just Learnt

How to evaluate any sustainability idea by asking 4 key questions: Time to implement? Cost required? Effort needed? Emissions impact towards RWH's goals?

When to Use This

  • Someone suggests a new idea
  • You're planning your department's goals
  • You need to explain priorities to leadership

Tips to Remember

  • Ask people from different departments
  • It's okay to change scores as you learn
  • Quick + cheap + helpful = great place to start!
